## üé® **Formatting Tips for PDF:**

### **Fonts:**
- **Title/Headings:** Bold, 14-16pt
- **Body Text:** Times New Roman, 12pt
- **Code Blocks:** Courier New, 10pt

### **Spacing:**
- **Line Spacing:** 1.5 or Double
- **Paragraph Spacing:** 6pt after
- **Margins:** 1 inch all sides

### **Header/Footer:**
- **Header:** Project title on even pages, Your name on odd pages
- **Footer:** Page numbers centered

### **Title Page:**
- Centered alignment
- Larger font for title (18-24pt)
- College logo (if required)
